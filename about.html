About

What is Story Board?

Story Board is a visual timeline-style archive for book series and standalone stories. Instead of full chapter text, each chapter is presented as a clean sequence of short summaries, paired with scene highlight images underneath.

The goal is to keep everything chronological and easy to follow — like flipping through a storyboard. It’s meant to feel calm, organized, and fun to revisit.

How the chapters work

Each chapter contains small event summaries in order, with no skipped moments in between. Under every summary is a matching scene highlight image to support the timeline visually.

This keeps the site lightweight and avoids including copyrighted book text, while still preserving the story structure in a storyboard format.
