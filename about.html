What is Story Board?

Story Board is a personal archive for standalone books, built as a calm visual “chapter map.” Each chapter is broken into short, chronological summaries, paired with scene images to make the story easy to revisit.

It’s designed to feel like flipping through a storyboard—quick to scan, easy to remember, and satisfying to browse.

How chapters are structured

Chapters are presented as a sequence of key story beats in order. Smaller moments are grouped into the closest major event, so each scene earns its place visually.

The focus is on clarity and progression: each summary moves the story forward, and each image represents a distinct moment.

Copyright & use

This site does not reproduce full book text. Summaries are written in my own words, and images are created as visual interpretations of scenes for personal organization and study.

All original books, characters, and story worlds remain the property of their respective authors and publishers. Story Board is not affiliated with any official publisher or release.
